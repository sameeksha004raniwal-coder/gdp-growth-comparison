# gdp-growth-comparison
A beginner Python project comparing GDP growth trends of India, China and the United States.
# GDP Growth Comparison: India, China and the United States

## Question
How have GDP growth rates of India, China and the United States changed over time?

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Data Source
World Bank Open Data

## What I Did
I collected GDP growth data, cleaned it using Python and created a line chart to compare growth patterns.

## Key Learning
GDP growth differs across countries depending on development stage, global shocks and policy choices.

## Limitation
GDP growth alone does not show inequality, employment quality, human development or environmental sustainability.
